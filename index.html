This chart provides a detailed breakdown of your swim stroke count over time, allowing you to analyze how your stroke technique evolves throughout the session. It's a great way to assess consistency and efficiency in swimming.

Visualization 2: Calories Burned vs Distance Over Time

This visualization highlights the relationship between calories burned and distance covered. It helps you track energy expenditure over your swims and see how distance and intensity contribute to your total calorie burn.
